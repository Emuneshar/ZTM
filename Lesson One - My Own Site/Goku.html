Son Goku!

About Son Goku

Son Goku, otherwise known as Goku, is the main character of our favorite anime. He's the hero who the story follows from the very first episode.

He starts off his journey as an alien child who is sent to Earth from a dying race of advanced alien realtors. A race of aliens called saiyans who buy and sell planets

As a child he was sent to Earth to prepare it for sale but got hit on the head as a baby and the rest is history. A martial arts prodigy Goku went from being a low born saiyan with limited potential to literally achieving the power of the gods.

One of the things that I admire the most about Goku is his impeccable work ethic and how he has a knack for turning enemies into friends. I believe there is something we can all learn from him.
